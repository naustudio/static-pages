Interactive Designer

  • Junior / Mid-level
  • Vacancies: 01

Job description

  • Design applications for Web/Mobile (all platforms)
  • Have knowlegde on User Experience and User Interface
  • Work closely and interactively with Developers and Art Director
  • Brainstorm and work with a Team
  • Love to take on challenges. Willing to learn is a must. There is nothing can grow without learning

Benefits

  • Competitive salary, medical insurance and other deserving benefits.
  • Creative, proactive and harmonious working environment.
  • Chances to learn and get advanced in career.

Requirements

  • Meticulous, and having a sharp eye for details
  • Having at least 1-year experience in designing web/mobile applications
  • Experienced in:
    • Photoshop (intermediate – advanced)
    • Illustration (intermediate – advance)
    • Flash (intermediate)
    • Dreamweaver (intermediate)
    • CSS & HTML/HTML5
  • Experience with video/audio editing softwares is a plus
  • Intermediate-advanced English skill is a must
  • Able to work well under pressure from project requirements and deadlines
  • Willing to work overtime when needed
  • Creative, willing to learn, able to research and adapt to new design trends
  • Able to face challenges
  • Passionate, self-motivated and a positive thinker
  • Good teamwork, get on well with team members
  • Good presentation skill and/or photographic skill is an advantage
